Show me the evidence!

Not everything is black and white. Show me the evidence! is a collaborative wiki-like site for comparing and sharing opinions on issues of any type. We promote data-centric discussion by only allowing comments in the context of external links to supporting evidence.

It's not about who's right or wrong, it's about understanding each other.
